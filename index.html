Welcome to The Old Stable

Description

The Old stable is a cosy guest house located right behind Chipping Sodbury High Street, where you'll find plenty of pubs, restaurants and shops.
This small two-floor guest house has everything you need for your stay on the edge of the Cotswolds and has a separate entrance through the garden.

About our space

On the ground floor you will find a kitchenette with the essentials for breakfast and a quick bite, a dining table and a double futon style sofa bed.
The bedroom (upstairs) has a double bed, wardrobe and a small bathroom.
Please note that the our the shower is quite short. If you are over 6 foot, it might be a tight squeeze!
The stairs are also quite narrow and relatively steep, so please consider this if you have any mobility issues.

Meet the team

The Old Stable is entirely managed by Gaia and Adam: we live at the same address and have recently renovated and decorated the space.
We will try our best to meet you when you arrive, but we also have a lock box so you can conveniently check-in (and check-out) at your convenience.
During your stay you might also meet our two purr-fect assistants, Freya and Nya!
